How To Tie Your Shoes

1. Hold your untied.

2. Cross the laces until a triangular gap is made.

3. Then pull one of the laces through the gap and pull.

4. Make a loop with one of the laces.

5. Throw over the other remaining lace over the loop until a gap is made.

6. Pull the thrown over lace through the gap and pull until a knot is made.
